Makaut : Palash’s Party

Palash was throwing a party this week for his awesome MAKAUT result. He invited all his college friends including girls and boys, seniors and juniors in the party. Puja being Palash’s best friend was invited in the party too. There were many people in the party who knew Palash, but they were not known by his other friends. So, they decided to talk to each other (1 unknown can talk to only 2 unknown friends and not all). While the introduction was going on Puja saw a pattern the people were following.

Every Girl in the party met a Girl first then a Boy whereas every Boy met a Boy first then a Girl. Puja analyzes this pattern and gets curious. Now she wants to know who will be the Nth person to in the Kth meeting. Can you help her?

 

Points to note:

·         Every person meets exactly 2 person (a boy and a girl).

·         The generation starts with a boy.

 

B       ------------------1st meeting

            /      \

          B          G -----------------2nd meeting

        /   \      /   \

      B     G   G     B

                     |

                  3rd person of 3rd meeting is a Girl

 

INPUT:

First line specifies T, the number of test cases.

The next T lines each gives 2 numbers, K and N.

 

OUTPUT:

Output 1 line for each test case giving the answer to Palash’s question.

Print “BOY” for boy, and “GIRL” for girl (quotes are only for clarification).

 

CONSTRAINTS:

1 ≤ T ≤ 10000

1 ≤ K ≤ 10000

1 ≤ N ≤ min(1015,2K-1)

 

INPUT

2

3 3

5 4

 

OUTPUT

GIRL

BOY
